About Us

The mission of Timmy Global Health is to expand access to healthcare and education, while empowering its members to engage in global development. Timmy Global Health has 48 chapters connected to international organizations. Each chapter funds projects and programs for health outreach. TIMMY is involved in global health by partaking in an annual international medical brigade. To prepare for this TIMMY UNC raises money and collects donations to fund the trip and pay for the medical supplies necessary to take on the brigade. Additionally, the organization recruits medical professionals to lead the international medical brigade each year.
