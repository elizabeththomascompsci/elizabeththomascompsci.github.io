Upcoming Events

Monthly Events:

Monthly service events take place around the Chapel Hill/Carrboro area. Specifics on these events will be communicated by email and at monthly TIMMY meetings.

Weekly Events:

Come help fundraise for TIMMY! We host trivia every Tuesday night at He's Not Here from 9-11 pm. It is $10 for a team of 5 and $2 for each additional player.

Annual Events:

Each year our TIMMY chapter sends participants on an international medical brigade. This year we will be attending Chonta Punta, Equador from August 2nd-11th. To find out more sign up for TIMMY and watch your email for upcoming events.
